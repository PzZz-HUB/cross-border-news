TikTok Celebrates Madonna’s Confessions II with Multi-part Global Campaign

Confessions II is set for release July 3rd via Warner Records“iHeartRadio and TikTok LIVE Premiere with Madonna” with an exclusive conversation and preview of new music from Confessions II, streaming LIVE July 2 on TikTok from the official album release party in LondonImmersive “TikTok House of Confessions” pop-ups in New York and London, featuring album listening experiences, creator-first content opportunities, and curated merchandise.Download Hi-Res Cover Art HERE / Photo Credit: Rafael PavarottiJune 29, 2026 (Los Angeles, CA) – Today, TikTok announced a multi-part global celebration with Madonna as anticipation reaches a fever pitch ahead of the release of her new album Confessions II out July 3 via Warner Records - bringing fans closer to the music, visuals, and the artist herself through exclusive content and experiences.On July 2, Madonna will join TikTok and iHeartRadio for the “iHeartRadio and TikTok LIVE Premiere with Madonna" from her album release party in London - a global event offering fans an exclusive first listen to Confessions II ahead of its July 3 release. Streaming worldwide on TikTok LIVE and iHeartRadio, the hour-long broadcast will see Madonna joined by Bob The Drag Queen for a candid conversation, providing a rare behind-the-scenes look at the creative process, inspirations and stories surrounding the new music. Airing one day before the album drops, Madonna will also give fans a preview of select songs ahead of the official release. Producer Stuart Price as well as Lola Leon - who collaborates with Madonna on the track “The Test” from Confessions II will also take part in the live stream. @madonna Join LIVE July 2 at 4:30pm ET for the iHeartRadio x TikTok LIVE Premiere - be the first to hear songs from CONFESSIONS II #iHeartMadonna #tiktoklivepremiere ♬ original sound - madonna Fans can tune in live on TikTok via @tiktok, @madonna, and @iheartradio, and engage in real time using TikTok features including polls, custom-designed “like” icons, fan Q&A, and live callouts integrated into the experience while Stuart Price, Honey Dijon, Jodie Harsh and Horse Meat Disco deliver nonstop music throughout the party. Register here.Additionally, fans can visit iHeartRadio’s digital station Hit Nation and use the talkback feature to share their confessions with Madonna beginning today until the show on July 2. The event will also broadcast on more than 200 iHeartRadio stations nationwide.The following day the dance floor opens from Friday, July 3 through July 4 with TikTok and Madonna’s immersive in-person pop-up experience and shop, “TikTok House of Confessions,” in both New York and London. Located at 188 Lafayette in New York City and The Vinyl Factory in London, each location will bring the energy of Confessions II to life through album-inspired visuals, curated merchandise including a limited-edition TikTok vinyl variant, exclusive listening moments, and Confessions-themed content creation opportunities.House of Confessions, London @ The Vinyl Factory is open on July 3 and 4 from 1:00 PM - 8:00 PMHouse of Confessions, New York @ 188 Lafayette is open on July 3 and 4 from 10:00 AM - 5:00 PMReservations are limited and available here: http://tiktokhouseofconfessions.citizenticket.com/TikTok will also launch a dedicated Confessions II in-app experience, giving fans worldwide new ways to engage with Madonna’s music and celebrate as a global fan community. Through interactive tasks, fan rewards, and profile frames, the experience pays homage to Madonna and the new album, allowing fans to dive deeper into the world of Confessions II and share their participation across TikTok.The campaign unites TikTok’s global community through the exclusive TikTok LIVE with iHeartRadio, an in-app fan experience, and immersive “House of Confessions” pop-ups in New York City and London. Together, these activations will invite fans to step inside the distinct visual world of Confessions II, offering new ways to discover the music, create, dance, and access exclusive merchandise in a way only TikTok can deliver.Madonna is one of the most beloved artists on TikTok. The campaign builds on her enduring presence on the platform, where her community of over 5.1M followers continues to rediscover, reinterpret, and celebrate her music. Following the announcement of the album, excitement is building across generations on TikTok, with fans embracing what’s being called a “Madonna Summer.”From trending sounds and iconic visuals to fashion inspiration and merchandise, TikTok has become a home for Madonna’s global fandom — and Confessions II marks a new opportunity for that community to come together both online and in person.
